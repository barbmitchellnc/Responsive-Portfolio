About Me

I'm excited to be in this class. I started out as an Industrial engineer supporting production plants. I rose into management and I helped close 12 of those plants while reporting to the Chief Operating Officer of the company.

When I had my son, I decided to make a change. I own a business that works with high IQ kids with learning disabilities. I have 2 kids and a twin sister in town - I'm sitting next to her husband in this class. My career path has had some twists and turns but I believe that makes life even more interesting.
